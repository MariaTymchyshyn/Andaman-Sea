About ThaiDiving

Phuket is a great place to get acquainted with diving or go to conquer the deep sea, if you are already an experienced diver. Our company has been working in Phuket for 10 years, and has gathered the best PADI instructors. We offer diving in Phuket for certified divers of various levels - from freshly trained Open Water divers to experienced technical and cave divers.

Explore Phuket

If you go on vacation to Phuket don't forget to save some time for scuba diving. The underwater world here is perfect both for amateurs and professional divers. Diving in Phuket and diving safaris on the Similan Islands are traditionally among the ten best places in the world for scuba diving. Phuket dive sites are famous for their variety of corals and an abundance of underwater fauna.

Scuba Diving Course

If you have already tried scuba diving, and you liked it, and you want to know more, be more independent under the water and dive in the places for certified divers, then the Open Water Diver diving course in Phuket is where you need to start. The course is divided into several parts: theory, practice, and of course, diving itself. It's simple: you go through all these parts of training in parallel: theory and practice in the pool or shallow water, and then real dives in the sea. During the training, you will learn everything that a diver should know: how the equipment works and how to use it and take care of it; where and when you can dive and how to plan your dives, how to swim under the water. You will learn so much that every day will bring you more and more vivid impressions!
